# Carnitas

- cumin
- salt
- black pepper
- 1 orange juice
    - a bit of zest, microplaned not much
- 2-3 bay leaves
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1 onion, diced
- dash cinnamon
- sugar
- molasses
- onion powder
- garlic powder
- chili powder
- 6-8 lbs pork shoulder or pork butt, cut into large chunks

Slow cook oven 3 hrs at 275F

Bake uncovered with a bit of broth at bottom and fat on top until crispy at 450F for 10-20 mins
